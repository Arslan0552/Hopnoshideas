THE REASON OF BLOGGING!!

Post by Aiman Malik, Jul 30, 2021.
Blogging Theme

It is estimated that 94% of B2B buyers and 81% of consumers do internet research prior to making a choice on what to buy. According to the findings of a research, the majority of the buyer’s journey now takes place online. To put it another way, your clients are educating themselves about the choices available to them via the use of independent resources such as the internet and blogs. If a company does not have a robust online presence and fails to attract consumers, the consequences may be disastrous for the company. Customers now turn to the internet as their major source of product information
